Welcome

Welcome to the official website of the Huntington Place Homeowners' Association, Inc. About

  • The Association exists to maintain our shared common areas and uphold community standards for the benefit of all homeowners.
  • Huntington Place Subdivision is a community of 87 homes (lots).
  • We are located in the Charter Township of Canton, Wayne County, Michigan.
  • The Association is governed by a Declaration of Covenants and Restrictions dated August 5, 1994, along with the bylaws of the Association.
